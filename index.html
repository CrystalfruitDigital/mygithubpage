My Goals


Simple. Clean. Powerful.
That is what I strive to achieve across my software applications and graphical designs. I believe that by following this mantra alongside using my skillset to blend aesthetics with utility, I will be able to best produce the valuable tools and visual assets that will empower my clientele.

I aim to use my experiences and connections to fuel future generations of visual artists and innovative scientists and am aiming to start a non-profit organization in the near future to accomplish just that. By connecting talented individuals with real businesses to create real products, I believe that I can help upcoming designers and developers just like myself to reach the their utmost potential.
