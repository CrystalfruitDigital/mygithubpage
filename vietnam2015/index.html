Day Zero : To New York

Monday, June 22nd, 2015

The prelude to our first family venture outside of the United States was a mad dash. We didn't prepare much and what we had already packed consisted of a 50 lb. bag of spam and a 50 lb bag of cereal and snacks. Needless to say, my family had to spend most of the morning cramming whatever we actually needed into the rest of the baggage.

As I finished packing my backpack my cousin and uncle came to whisk us off to Philadelphia where a bus would take us to the John F. Kennedy International Airport in New York.
